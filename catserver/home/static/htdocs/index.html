Features

Service Apps

This service gateway manipulates all connectable IoT devices with service applications. We support various service apps and software components that can be created by combining with all IoT devices around you. Just select app you want, that's it.

Stand-alone

Service does not perform on Cloud-based Platform(Optional, but not ready yet). It works stand-alone on your local area network. It means you do not have to pay for network service. You only need to buy a license once for a service application.

Open Source

COSSB(Component-based Open & Simple Service Broker) and its framework support for building a service component and application. Developer can freely use, modify, redistribute our framework and components under the simplified BSD license(BSD 3-Clause).

Solutions

Some specific IoT solution you desired may require various functionalities. We will provide examples and suggest some packages by experimental service scenario to build your IoT business.

Compatible

Software framework and all components are also can be performed on the Open Hardware Platform, which supports Linux OS, such as RPi, Odroid, Edison, etc.

Service Oriented

We aim to build an IoT service system and its framework that can round up the individual or independent single services for more intelligent IoT service.
